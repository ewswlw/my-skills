## Deviations

- Plan called for a separate "free resource / lead magnet" as a background E-phase task.
  During implementation, this was elevated to a first-class section with full template
  content, because the checklist itself functions as an outreach opener — deviation improves
  the deliverable.

- Plan mentioned "optional" LinkedIn paid ads in months 5–6. In the final GTM plan,
  this was reframed as a conditional decision gate with explicit criteria, not just "optional."
  This adds decision clarity for the founder.

- Added a "Week 1 Immediate Actions" section not explicitly in the plan — the research phase
  flagged that the founding team needs to start pilot conversion immediately. This addition
  prevents the common trap of over-planning before acting.
